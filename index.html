Ng Dry Cleaners

Keeping Curaçao's wardrobe clean since 1962

We are Ng Dry Cleaners

After opening the laundry July of 1962, Sau Kim Ng lost her husband - but not her hope. Also known to many as Señora Ng, she continued to work tirelessly as an entrepeneur, as a widow, and as a mother.
After her death in 2016, her children continued running the laundry with the same spirit and quality their mother did. Sau Kim continues to inspire the laundry today by using quality products and family to keep Curaçao's clothing sharp.
